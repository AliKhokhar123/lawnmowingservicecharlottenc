Why Foxcroft Lawns Need Special Care

Foxcroft lawns have some special needs because of the area's soil and weather:

  • The soil in Foxcroft can sometimes be clay-heavy, which means water doesn't drain well
  • Large trees in the neighborhood create shady areas where some grass types don't grow well
  • Hot Charlotte summers can stress grass if it's not watered and cut properly

Our team knows how to handle these challenges to keep your lawn looking its best.

Our Lawn Care Schedule for Foxcroft Homes

  • Fall - Leaf removal, fall fertilization, overseeding if needed
  • Winter - Clean up, prepare lawn for cold weather, plan for spring
