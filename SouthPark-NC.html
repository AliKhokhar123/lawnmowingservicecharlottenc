SouthPark NC Lawn Care Tips

Living in SouthPark means your lawn has special needs:

  • Water your grass early in the morning to save water
  • Cut your grass to about 3 inches tall in summer
  • In fall, don't leave piles of leaves on your lawn
  • In spring, remove dead grass to help new grass grow
  • Plant grass seed in September for the best results
