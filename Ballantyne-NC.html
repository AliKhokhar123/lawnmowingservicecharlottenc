Ballantyne Lawn Care Tips From Our Experts

Ballantyne has clay soil that can make growing grass tricky. Here are some simple tips that work well in our area::

  • Water your lawn deeply but not too often (1-2 times per week is better than every day)
  • Mow at the right height (3-4 inches is best for most Ballantyne lawns)
  • Add lawn food (fertilizer) in spring and fall
  • Remove leaves promptly in fall to keep your grass healthy
  • Get your soil tested every few years
